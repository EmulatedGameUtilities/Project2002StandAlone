Guild Management Tips

- Click the tabs to display organized data about your guildmembers.
- To sort the guild member list, just click on the column header you wish to sort on. All tabs will be show the player list sorted by the last column clicked.
- You can "dump" your guild information into a text file for later use. Pushing the Dump button creates a text file in your EQ directory, named after your guild. You can also use the /outputfile command to specify a filename to dump to. Type "/outputfile" by itself for usage instructions.

Roster tab tips
- To Promote a member, you must have the guild member targetted, as the member must be in the same zone to promote him.
- To change the Message of the Day (MOTD) (Officers and Leader only), click in the text field containing the current MOTD, modify it, and click the Update button.

Notes tab tips
- Public Notes are visible to everyone in the guild. Only guild leaders and officers can edit these notes.
- Private Notes are only visible and editable to you.

Tribute tab tips
- Active flag - The "active" status of a member describes whether that member is participating in the Guild Tribute Benefits selected for your Guild. Note that, even if you have opted-in, you cannot receive Guild Tribute benefits while in Shroud form. Your "active" status should show in yellow while in Shroud form, and your guild will not be charged since you won't be receiving the benefits.
- Opting out of Tribute - You can opt yourself out of Guild Tribute on the Tribute tab by selecting yourself in the list and clicking the Opt Out button. Similarly, guild leaders and officers can perform this action on anyone in the guild (even if they're offline).
- Opting in to Tribute - You can only opt-in to Guild Tribute at the Guild Tribute Master. You can find one of these in your Guild Hall.

For more information, please see the Guild Management section of Help.
