2.4.2 The Consider (/Con) System

The /Con system allows a player to consider the level of difficulty of an opponent. To consider something, right-click on your target. You may also left-click on your target, then type /con. When you consider something, you will see one of seven colored messages in your chat window. Below is a explanation of the "con" colors.

* Gray - This target is significantly below your skill level and offers no experience.

* Green - This target is below your skill level and offers reduced experienced.

* Blue - This target is somewhat below your skill level.

* Dark Blue - This target is an adequate challenge and offers an experience bonus.

* White - This target is a challenge of equivalent skill and offers an experience bonus.

* Yellow - This target is more skilled than you and offers an experience bonus.

* Red - This target is far more skilled than you and offers an experience bonus.
