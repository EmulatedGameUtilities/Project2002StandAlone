Smithing. The Highest of Str/Wis/Int will affect your skill up rate.<br>
<br>
eqtraders.com trade supply locations. Get used to using this link<br>
<br>
This formula seems to work for smithing<br>
success = skill - (0.75 * triv) + 51.5<br>
<br>
Skill Mod Items (advise you skill to 195 to see if smithing is really a skill you want before going for the 10% and 15% items)<br>
geerlok automated hammer (tinkered): +5% smithing<br>
grandmaster smith's hammer (player made): +5% smithing<br>
Massive girdle of the forge (drop in Sanctus Seru): +10% smithing<br>
Hammer of the ironfrost (drop off Xerkizh the Creator in Temple of Ssraeshza): +15% smithing<br>
Gauntlets of dark embers (drop off Kaas Thox Xi Ans Dyex in Vex Thal): +15% smithing<br>
Vah Shir Warrior cloaks from their smithing quests<br>

An alternate is Mistletoe Cutting Sickle Blade till 183, Its quite cheaper in plat and has the benefit of being an item needed for 222+ smithing.<br>
<br>
Blessed Sickle Blade = Sheet Metal + Smithy Hammer + Emerald + Curved Blade Mold + Celestial Essence + Blessed Dust of Tunare + Mistletoe Temper (forge, smithing trivial 183)<br>
Celestial Essence = 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 High End Research component (mortar and pestle, no-fail combine)<br>
Mistletoe Temper = 1 Celestial Essence + 1 Elven Wine + 1 Mistletoe (brew barrel, brewing trivial 136)<br>
Blessed Dust of Tunare = 1 Imbued Emerald + 1 Celestial Essence + 1 Jar of Acid (kiln, pottery trivial 103)<br>
