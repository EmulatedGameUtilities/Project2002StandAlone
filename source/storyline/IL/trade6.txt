Jewelcraft. Stat to gain Skill is the Highest of Int or Wis. <br>
<br>
Cost.. About 5K <br>
Time.. About one week (if you enchant the bars) <br>
VITAL - Get a 5% skill Mod (Geerloc Gemsetter) <br>
Enchant all bars from skill 192 up. If the bars are enchanted you will be able to sell for more then the cost of components (I almost broke even) <br>
You do have an enachanter willing to enchant 100 stacks of bars? Don't proceed unless you do or are willing to lose a few K plat. <br>
Get Charisma to 130 for Buying or selling. <br>
